Frozen The Broadway Musical

Duration: 2h 15m | Intermission: 1 | Opened: March 22, 2018 | Audience: Ages 8 & up

Summary

Frozen is the timeless tale of two sisters, pulled apart by a mysterious secret. As one young woman struggles to find her voice and harness her powers within, the other embarks on an epic adventure to bring her family together once and for all. Both are searching for love. They just don't know where to find it.

Parent Information

The smash hit Frozen is now a Broadway hit! Frozen on Broadway keeps the movie's magical charm and wonderful story and is a wonderful musical to take children too. However it is good to keep in mind that sitting still and quite for more than an hour could be difficult for some children. Keeping that in mind our age recomedation for Frozen is 8+ while saying its content is suitable for children as young as 4.

Audience

FROZEN is a full-length Broadway musical running over 2 hours. FROZEN on Broadway is intended for ages 8+ and is not recommended for anyone under the age of 4.
